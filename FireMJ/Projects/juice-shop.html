OWASP Juice Shop – Pentest Case Study

🛡 Ethical Hacking · OWASP Top 10 · 4 Critical Findings

Overview

A simulated e‑commerce platform riddled with vulnerabilities. I executed a full penetration test to uncover exploitable flaws, assess their business impact, and recommend targeted remediations.

Methodology

Performed reconnaissance, mapped application structure, ran automated scans with OWASP ZAP, and manually exploited inputs to confirm vulnerabilities. Documented each step with evidence.

Key Findings / Outcomes

SQL InjectionCritical – exposed customer dataParameterized queries & input sanitization
